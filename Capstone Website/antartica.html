Antartica
Emperor Penguin
>
One of the most popular animals from this continent, the emperor penguin is most well known for its black back and white belly. They use their skin coloring as camouflage to hide from unsuspecting fish and predators that may be looking down on them while they are swimming. Right now these birds are considered to be near threatened due to habitat destruction via global warming and overfishing.
Light-Mantled Albatross
>
A darker grey bird that is mostly a surface ocean fisher for crustaceans, squid, and of course fish. Using their massive wingspans, they are also able to glide for hundreds of miles without flapping. Now they are considered to be near threatened due to overfishing in the area where they live.
