This page is specifically about birds from Africa...

Peregrine Falcon

This falcon is the largest in Africa and the fastest animal in the world with diving speeds over 200mph. It likes to live in high up perches, whether that be telephone poles or mountains. Generally they like to eat smaller birds like pigeons and or ducks. As a relatively large bird of prey, it is surprising that overhunting doesn’t occur, but the population of this bird is stable.

Knysna Warbler

Sort of a mouse-like bird in its stature, it’s a rather small bird living in South Africa, feeding on insects, worms, and other such creatures. Due to the loss and degradation of their habitat their population is incredibly fractured with only a few groups here and there, the species is considered to be vulnerable.
