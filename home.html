Help Save the Birds!
MILLIONS of Birds Die
Each Year...
…and most of them are due to human causes not surprisingly. According to the USDA Forest Service, more than 500 million birds are killed every year due to human activities. Collisions with windows, electrocution by power lines, and the use of pesticides in farming all account for a few of the things that lead up to those deaths. That is not to say there aren’t bigger factors however…
Enviromental Pollution
Oil spills, over-fishing, habitat destruction, and poaching are among the deadliest. With losses to their food supply and homeland, many bird species are dwindling towards extinction with illegal poaching not making the situation any better.
But There is Hope...
Due to conservation efforts around the globe in forms such as fundraising for sanctuaries and the passing of laws preventing hunting of endangered birds and/or over-hunting, many of these birds have been brought back from near extinction. Despite all the progress we have made there is still work to be done.
